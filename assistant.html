An assistant you actually own.

Cottage Assistant is a personal AI assistant designed to live on your own machine, not in someone else’s cloud. It helps with everyday questions, reminders, notes, and small tasks while keeping your data, memory, and conversations where they belong: with you.

Personal computing should still feel personal.

Computers were once imagined as tools that belonged to the people using them: practical, flexible, and close at hand. Over time, much of that has been replaced by subscriptions, platforms, and software that works only by sending more of your life elsewhere.

Cottage Assistant is an attempt to recover some of what was lost. It is built around a simple belief: your assistant should be part of your own computing life, not a rented service standing between you and your machine.

Private by design

Your conversations, reminders, notes, and context stay with you. Cottage Assistant is meant to work locally first, so your personal computing can remain personal.

Owned, not rented

Your assistant should not be another monthly dependency. Cottage Assistant is being built around the idea that useful software can belong to the person using it.

Calm and useful

Not a spectacle. Not a platform. Not a dashboard full of noise. Just a helpful tool for everyday life: questions, reminders, memory, and small acts of assistance.

Built for ordinary use

The goal is not an AI toy for hobbyists, but a tool that can be downloaded, run, and used without needing to assemble an entire technical ecosystem around it.

A different idea of an assistant

Cottage Assistant is not meant to be a giant remote authority. It is meant to be your assistant: something that understands your notes, remembers what matters to you, and helps with the ordinary texture of daily life.

The aim is a quieter kind of software — something closer to a household tool than a platform. An assistant that lives with you, on your machine, and works on your behalf.

What Cottage Assistant stands for

  • Local-first by default
  • Privacy without theatrics
  • Software you can actually own
  • Memory and assistance grounded in your life
  • A calmer alternative to subscription-driven AI

Some things should still belong to you.

Cottage Assistant is being built in that spirit: as a personal tool for personal computing, shaped around usefulness, ownership, and trust.

Your assistant should live with you, not in someone else’s cloud.
